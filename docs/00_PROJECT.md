# DocumentaryFactory

Version: 1.0 (Foundation)

---

# Vision

DocumentaryFactory is an AI-powered desktop application designed to automate the production of professional faceless documentary videos for YouTube.

Instead of manually managing dozens of disconnected tools, DocumentaryFactory combines scripting, timeline generation, storyboard planning, asset management, voice synchronization, video composition, and exporting into one integrated workflow.

The goal is to allow a creator to produce high-quality documentary videos quickly while maintaining consistent visual identity and professional production standards.

---

# Mission

Build a complete AI-assisted documentary production platform that transforms ideas into publish-ready videos.

Input:

- Topic
- Script
- Subtitle (.srt)
- Narration
- Images
- Video assets

Output:

A fully edited documentary video ready for YouTube.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. Local First

The software must work completely offline.

Cloud AI services are optional.

Users always own their files.

---

## 2. AI Assisted

AI helps the creator.

AI never replaces creative decisions.

Every generated result can be edited manually.

---

## 3. Modular

Every component is independent.

Examples:

Timeline Engine

Asset Library

Storyboard

Export

Voice Sync

Character Manager

can all evolve separately.

---

## 4. Free First

Priority is given to free and open-source software.

Paid cloud AI services are optional.

---

## 5. Scalability

The architecture must support future expansion without rewriting the system.
